What is SNPTZ?

SNPTZ is an email-based productivity tool. SNPTZ is not a to-do list or ticket tracker or calendar. We think its an easy way to set goals, reflect on accomplishments, keep yourself honest, and stay focused. SNPTZ can also provide a painless way for colleagues to stay infomed about what everyone else is up to and how other projects are going. We like to think of the team features like a weekly stand-up/scrum meeting that you can attend from wherever you can access your inbox.

How do I use SNPTZ?

1. On Monday morning, you get an email from SNPTZ. 2. You hit reply and do two things: - Review your goals from last week and edit them to reflect what you actually worked on last week. Remember: its not a to-do list. Stuff Happens. Things change. Don't feel bad if you didn't work on what you said you would last week. This week is a new week. - List a few things you are planning on working on this week. - FYI - please don't get creative with your email reply's format -- our parsing is crap and your ASCII art might not come out right. 3. If you are a member of any SNPTZ teams with your colleagues, you'll receive a digest email Monday afternoon. The digest contains all of your colleages' lists of things they'll be working on this week. 4. Visit snptz.com to browse your esteemed colleagues and see what they actually worked on last week.

What if I mess up my email?

Don't panic. You have options. 1. Send another reply -- it will replace whatever your previous email contained. 2. Log into snptz.com -- you can click this week's task or older tasks to edit them.

Who makes SNPTZ? Why?

SNPTZ is made by Ted and Evan. Ted used a similar (proprietary) thing at a large organization and found it valuable -- now he wants a similar tool to use at a smaller organization.. Evan's small, distributed team tries doing weekly stand-ups via email, but its clunky and noisy (lots of reply-alls).

I can haz mine SNPTZ?

Yes! SNPTZ is written in Python. The SNPTZ source code is free and open source (New BSD). Please fork on github so we can easily benefit from the fruits of your labor. SNPTZ is a side project, so contributions are very welcome! Currently, the code only runs on Google App Engine. We'll port it to Django one of these weeks...
